IT ethics is important because technology affects people’s privacy, safety, and daily lives. As technology continues to grow, professionals need to make responsible decisions when designing and using systems. Ethical issues in IT include data privacy, security, fairness, and accountability. Companies collect large amounts of user data, so they must ensure that this data is protected and not misused. If systems are not secure, users can face risks such as identity theft or data breaches.

Guidelines

Protect user data and ensure privacy.
Be honest and transparent about how systems work.
Avoid bias in algorithms and decision-making.
Build secure and reliable systems.
Take responsibility for the impact of technology.
Be honest
Avoid bias
Keep systems secure
